# Code of Conduct

AsyncViz is committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, inclusive, and professional community for everyone who participates in the project. We want every contributor, reviewer, and user to feel that their participation is valued, regardless of background or experience level.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive community include:

- Being respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Welcoming contributors with different backgrounds, perspectives, and levels of experience.
- Giving and accepting feedback professionally, and assuming good intent.
- Focusing discussion on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy, patience, and professionalism toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Personal attacks or insults directed at other contributors.
- Discriminatory language or behavior, including content that demeans others based on personal characteristics.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Offensive, abusive, or sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing another person's private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional open-source setting.

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They will:

- Provide clear standards of acceptable behavior.
- Respond to reported violations fairly and consistently.
- Take appropriate corrective action when necessary.
- Help maintain a respectful environment in which contributors can collaborate productively.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- GitHub Discussions
- Project documentation
- Community communication channels related to AsyncViz
- Any public spaces in which an individual is officially representing the project

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- A private warning.
- A request to modify or remove specific content.
- Temporary restrictions on participation in the project.
- Permanent removal from the project community.

Every report will be reviewed fairly, investigated appropriately, and handled as confidentially as is reasonably possible. Maintainers are committed to responding consistently and proportionately to the nature of each incident.
